Visual Information-Project

Visualisation of football players data

Made by Georgij Li and Alexandre Sprich
Show data for:
1) Position prediction for football players based on attribute values
Prediction was made with KNN algorithm for k=3. The color of a line represents the predicted position for a certain player.
Hover over a line to get player specific information and their real life position.
Striker (ST/CF) Winger (LM/LW/LF/RM/RW/RF) Attacking midfielder (CAM) Central midfielder (CM)
Defensive midfielder (CDM) Full-back (LB/LWB/RB/RWB) Centre-back (CB)

2) Relation between age and pace of the players, to see how age affects football players' pace.
Hover over circles to get player specific information.
